# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability
We take the security of our tools seriously. If you find a vulnerability in `p9`, please do NOT open a public issue.

1. Email us at **security@p9labs.io**
2. Include steps to reproduce the issue.

## Our Process
* **Capacity:** We investigate reports based on availability and capacity. We do not guarantee immediate response times.
* **Coordinated Disclosure:** Please do not share information about a vulnerability until a fix is released.
